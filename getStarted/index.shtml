Booking Events Production for your event is easy! Following these three basic steps will ensure that we can provide you with the best experience possible.

1. Reserve Your Event Space

Before you can book EP services, you must first book the venue where you will be hosting the event. Events Production currently provides services to all venues on the Fairfax campus. Each of these venues can be reserved through the Office of Events Management (OEM). You can also check out the university’s online scheduling program, 25Live, for venue availability and reservations. Once you receive confirmation from OEM you can continue to the next step(s).

2. Review Event Policies and Guidelines

Events at George Mason are subject to several policies and guidelines which are in place to ensure safe and comfortable sharing of venues on a thriving academic campus. These policies include noise ordinances, space restrictions, and hosting procedures. Please take a few moments to review these policies and guidelines by following the links below:

  • GMU Noise Ordinances
  • Student Centers Event Blah
  • Late Night Event Procedures
  • Sean’s McAwesomeville
